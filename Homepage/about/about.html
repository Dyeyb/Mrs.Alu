WHO WE ARE

Cal Elite Builders & Cal Electrical — established in 2004 in Lishui Town, Nanhai District, Foshan — has grown over two decades into one of Guangdong's most trusted full-case interior aluminum solutions providers.

Today, our state-of-the-art extrusion production lines, precision spraying facilities, and dedicated technical team serve contractors, interior designers, and property developers across China.

Our History

Two Decades of
Building With Purpose.

Founded in Lishui Town, Nanhai District, Foshan in 2004, Cal Elite began as a specialist in aluminum skirting boards. What started as a focused, single-product operation grew steadily through smart investment in technology and people — until today, our 30 Mu facility houses some of the most advanced extrusion and spraying equipment in the region.

Our expansion into a full-case interior solutions provider — adding wall panels, grille plates, wainscoting, metal decorative lines, and more — came directly from listening to the contractors and designers who trusted us most.
