Company Overview

Built on Two Decades
of Mastery

Founded in 2004 in Lishui Town, Nanhai, Foshan — the aluminum capital of China — Cal Elite has grown from a dedicated manufacturer into a full-solution provider for premium interior aluminum systems. Our Builders and Cal Electrical divisions deliver advanced extrusion and precision finishing that stand apart from the ordinary.
