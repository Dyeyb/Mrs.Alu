Who We Are

Foshan's
Leading Name
in Aluminum.

Foshan Aluminum Lady Building Materials Co., Ltd. — known across the industry as Mrs.Alu — was established in 2004 in Lishui Town, Nanhai District, Foshan. From a focused workshop producing aluminum skirting boards, we have grown over two decades into one of Guangdong's most trusted full-case interior aluminum solutions providers.

Today, our state-of-the-art extrusion production lines, precision spraying facilities, and dedicated technical team serve contractors, interior designers, and property developers across China — delivering premium aluminum wainscoting, wall panels, metal decorative lines, grille plates, and much more.

Our History

Two Decades
of Building
With Purpose.

Founded in Lishui Town, Nanhai District, Foshan in 2004, Mrs.Alu began as a specialist in aluminum skirting boards. What started as a focused, single-product operation grew steadily through smart investment in technology and people — until today, our 30 Mu facility houses some of the most advanced extrusion and spraying equipment in the region.

Our expansion into a full-case interior solutions provider — adding wall panels, grille plates, wainscoting, metal decorative lines, and more — came directly from listening to the contractors and designers who trusted us most.
