Welcome to Mrs.Alu

Premium Aluminum Building Materials & Interior Solutions

Foshan Aluminum Lady Building Materials Co., Ltd. was founded in Lishui Town, Nanhai, Foshan in 2004. We specialize in aluminum wainscoting, wall panels, metal decorative lines, and skirting boards. With advanced extrusion production lines, spraying facilities, and a dedicated team of technical professionals, we are committed to providing innovative, high-quality interior solutions that transform spaces with elegance and durability.
