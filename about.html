      Company Introduction
      Foshan Aluminum Lady Building Materials Co., Ltd.
      Foshan Aluminum Lady Building Materials Co., Ltd. was founded in 2004 in the famous "Foshan Aluminum Town" - Lishui Town, Nanhai, Foshan. The company has extrusion production lines, spraying lines, and outstanding technical and service talents. Aluminum Lady adheres to the corporate culture and service concept of "innovation, quality, and sharing", focusing on ceiling and wall panel full-case solution service provider, wall panel, metal decorative lines, skirting board research and development, mold opening, production and sales; widely used in hotels, restaurants, KTV, schools, home decoration and other places, with excellent product quality and people-oriented marketing concept, it has established Aluminum Lady's brand position in the industry.
      We follow the business tenet of "focusing on quality" and strictly require all departments of the company to concentrate on their work as the forefront of the industry. We sincerely provide customers with fast and high-quality services and offer extra value to every customer. For a long time, Mrs. Aluminum has always adhered to the tenet of excellent quality, thoughtful service, and the spirit of excellence, and will work with you to create brilliance together!
